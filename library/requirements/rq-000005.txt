>>>requirement.rq-000005

title:: Minimize Presentation Markup

is_part_of:: rq-000107

priority:: routine

type:: data format

description:: The data format shall minimize document
[g:SML:presentation] markup.  The primary purpose of this requirement
is to limit authors capability to adjust presentation. In other words,
~~deny~~ authors the ability to specify the ~~style~~ or
~~appearance~~ of their content.

revision:: $Revision: 11687 $

date:: $Date: 2012-12-06 13:59:01 -0700 (Thu, 06 Dec 2012) $

author:: $Author: don.johnson $

index:: rq-000005

Authors should focus on creating content instead of fiddling with
document presentation.  Modern documents are often published to
multiple formats and authors often do not control how their content is
presented to readers.

outcome::2015-12-12:rq-000005:green: This requirement is complete and
understandable.
