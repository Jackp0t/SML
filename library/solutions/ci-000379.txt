description:: The [g:sml:SML] syntax defines the rules governing the
expression of statements and combinations of statements used to
represent the content of an SML document. The SML syntax specification
is a context free grammar implemented in [g:EBNF].  The SML syntax is
not customizable and applies the same to all SML libraries.
